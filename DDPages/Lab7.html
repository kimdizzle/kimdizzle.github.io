Cindy Lou

But, unlike her other counterparts, Cindy Lou believes there is some good in the Grinch. Fun Facts

  1. Dr. Seuss’ book was originally published in 1957 by Random House
  2. The original Grinch was not green, he was black and white with pink eyes
  3. Dr. Seuss was 53 years old when the book was written
  4. In the original story, the Grinch lives 3,000 feet up Mount Crumpet. However, in the special, it’s 10,000 feet up
  5. The live-action film cost more than $115 million to make
  6. Jim Carrey’s Grinch costume was made up of yak hairs that were dyed green and sewn on to a spandex suit
  7. Although most of the character designs were based on the book, Dr. Seuss thought the Grinch looked like Jones
  8. Dr. Seuss wrote the lyrics to all of the songs in the television special
  9. Dr. Seuss was initially concerned about Karloff’s casting because he was worried he would make the Grinch scary
  10. The line, “You’re a rotter, Mr. Grinch” was censored for years because it was deemed too vulgar for television
